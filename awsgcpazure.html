AWS, GCP, and Azure

The main difference between Cloud Computing and traditional design is Horizontal Scaling. You have to increase and decrease capacity when you have a traditional website. Once you need to scale down, you have a bunch of unnecessary servers that you paid for. AWS- Pick your region, pick your availability zone. When you are starting in AWS, you set it up in the region that you think the most traffic will come from. EC2- Cloud computing with auto scaling that can add and delete servers. S3 is huge storage. Microsoft Azure- Microsoft is the premiere enterprise technology solution provider. By leveraging their existing install base Microsoft makes cloud migration simple. Microsoft uses the tools and platforms that millions of developers already rely on.You can easily integrate and deploy NodeJS. Google has been doing this for over 15 years and custom build everything they do. Google has the fastest global network and are opening their infrastructure through the cloud platform.
